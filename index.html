Mentorship and Dissertation

Doctoral students need to perform as effective protégées of their doctoral mentors during their doctoral studies and research. At the same time, as collaborators of masters and undergraduate students, doctoral students ought to familiarize themselves with and practice key elements of mentoring skills, styles, personalities, etc. Such an experience will allow them to become effective mentors in their academic or industry careers upon graduation. Thus, we organize seminars and workshops, conducted by experts, that can help doctoral students learn, practice, and hone their skills to be effective mentees and mentors.
